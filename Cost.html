Tuition and Fees
  • Undergraduate tuition: Approximately $17,000 per year (in-state), $36,000 (out-of-state)
  • Graduate tuition: Approximately $22,000 per year (in-state), $36,000 (out-of-state)
  • Student fees: Estimated at $1,500 annually
  • Cost per credit hour: $1,500 for undergraduate and $1,800 for graduate students

Financial Aid Options
  • Merit based scholarships: Available for high-achieving students
  • Need based financial aid: Based on FAFSA results
  • Federal Direct Loans: Subsidized and unsubsidized options
  • State grants: New Jersey State Grant programs for eligible students
  • Work study programs: Part-time jobs for students to help pay educational expenses

Payment Plans and Deadlines
  • Payment plans: Options available to spread tuition payments over the semester
  • Fall tuition payment deadline: Typically in mid-August
  • Spring tuition payment deadline: Typically in early January
  • Refund policy: Full refund if withdrawal occurs before the start of classes
